Litti Chokha

Litti Chokha is a traditional Bihari dish made of roasted wheat balls stuffed with sattu, served with spiced mashed vegetables.

Thekua

Thekua is a sweet, deep-fried cookie made from wheat flour, jaggery, and ghee, popular during Chhath Puja.

Khaja

Khaja is a crispy, layered sweet pastry soaked in sugar syrup, commonly made in Bihar.

Dal Pitha

Dal Pitha is a Bihari delicacy made of rice flour dumplings stuffed with spiced lentils, served with chutney or curry.
